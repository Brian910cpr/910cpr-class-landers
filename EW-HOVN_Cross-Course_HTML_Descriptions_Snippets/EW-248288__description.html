American Red Cross - Basic Life Support (BLS) The American Red Cross Basic Life Support (BLS) Provider course is designed for healthcare professionals and first responders who need to develop or refresh their life-saving skills in a structured, hands-on environment. This instructor-led course delivers high-performance CPR training with a strong focus on team-based response, real-world scenarios, and hands-on skill practice. It meets industry standards for emergency care training and ensures participants gain the confidence needed to respond quickly and effectively to cardiac and breathing emergencies.
