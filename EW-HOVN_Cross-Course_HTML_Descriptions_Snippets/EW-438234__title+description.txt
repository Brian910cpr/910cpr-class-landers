AHA Instructor Trio: BLS, ACLS & PALS

This all-inclusive instructor course is designed for experienced healthcare providers who are ready to become certified instructors in Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS), and Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS) through the American Heart Association (AHA).
Participants must hold valid provider-level certifications in all three disciplines (BLS, ACLS, and PALS) prior to enrollment. The course includes in-depth instruction on AHA teaching methodology, course delivery standards, equipment management, and skills testing practices.
