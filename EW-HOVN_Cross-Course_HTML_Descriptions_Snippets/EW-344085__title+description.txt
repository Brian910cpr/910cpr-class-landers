This class is for any individual who is looking for self-improvement, basic CPR for work that does not require a First Aid component (although, if desired, First Aid can be added by registering HERE), or families looking to ensure safety in the house and around the pool area.
What is covered

Adult, child and infant CPR
Foreign-body airway obstruction
Automated External Defibrillator (AED)

Plan on spending <2 hours of interactive instruction and learning
 
Price Includes:

Education and interactive skills instruction adult, child, and infant CPR with AED
American Heart Association Course completion card for Heartsaver CPR for 2 years.
