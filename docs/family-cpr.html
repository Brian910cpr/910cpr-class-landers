What the course covers

This American Heart Association course covers:

  • Hands-Only CPR
  • CPR with breaths
  • AED use
  • choking relief for adults, children, and infants
  • infant CPR
More practice, less pressure.

The course uses guided practice and group instruction so participants spend more time actually performing the skills.

Good for

Parents, grandparents, babysitters, caregivers, community groups, and anyone who wants to be better prepared for an emergency.

Important

Family & Friends CPR is an educational course and does not include a certification card.
