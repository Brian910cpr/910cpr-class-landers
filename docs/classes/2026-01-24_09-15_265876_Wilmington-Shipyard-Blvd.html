❤️ Become a Heartsaver® Instructor

So, you’re ready to teach CPR and First Aid? Let’s make it official.

As an AHA Heartsaver® Instructor, you’ll be able to offer training in:

  • CPR & AED use

  • First Aid

  • Pediatric First Aid CPR/AED

  • K–12 School Programs

  • Bloodborne Pathogens

  • ...and more.

    • You’ll have the freedom to:

    • Teach at your own workplace or anywhere else you’re approved

    • List your classes on the official AHA class locator

    • Certify students across the U.S.

      • ✅ What's Included

      Your registration includes:

      • Heartsaver Instructor Essentials (online course)

      • Heartsaver Instructor Manual (digital or print, shipped to you)

      • In-person Instructor Course with a live facilitator

      • Initial Instructor Monitoring within 6 months

      • Alignment with our AHA training site (optional but available)

        • 🧾 What You Might Still Need

        • Current AHA Heartsaver Provider Certification – required before your instructor status can be finalized. If you don’t have one yet, you can add the provider course during registration.

        • Heartsaver Student Workbook – needed only if completing your provider course as part of this process.

          • 🧭 What to Expect

          1. Complete the Instructor Essentials online course (link emailed after registration)

          2. Attend your Instructor Course Day

          3. Complete your monitored session within 6 months of your course

            4. 🚀 Teach Where You Want, Get Support When You Need It

            Whether you plan to teach through your job, your own business, or as an independent trainer — we’ll help you launch strong. No micromanagement. No weird obligations. Just helpful resources and a supportive team.

            AHA digital products are non-refundable.
