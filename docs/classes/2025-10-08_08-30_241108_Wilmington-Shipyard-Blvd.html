AHA - ACLS Provider – In-person

The Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) course is designed for healthcare professionals involved in the management of cardiopulmonary emergencies and resuscitation. This in-person, instructor-led training follows American Heart Association guidelines and emphasizes high-performance team dynamics and clinical decision-making in critical situations.

This course is intended for personnel in emergency response, emergency medicine, intensive care, and critical care units. You may add an optional BLS renewal during registration if both certifications are needed.

Course Topics Include:

  • Early recognition and management of respiratory and cardiac arrest

  • Airway management and related pharmacology

  • Post-cardiac arrest care and team communication

  • Identification and treatment of acute coronary syndromes and stroke

    • Course Includes:

    • Instructor-led classroom training with video-based and scenario-driven instruction

    • Hands-on station practice and skills testing

    • American Heart Association ACLS Provider eCard (valid for 2 years)
