STOP THE BLEED® Interactive Course

Stop The Bleed is a national campaign that aims to make bleeding control training as commonplace as CPR training. Since a person can bleed out and die in as little as three minutes it is literally a matter of life or death that someone take action immediately, someone who is already there. Remember, until help arrives, YOU are the help.

This interactive course is a virtual alternative to the in-person lecture presentation. The STOP THE BLEED® Interactive Course guides individuals through the three methods of bleeding control using video demonstrations, interactive learning, and spontaneous quizzes.

910CPR believes strongly that this information is valuable and want to bring this free program to your attention.  The online STOP THE BLEED® Interactive Course is free to all and can be taken multiple times to learn and refresh knowledge of the material. 

Although the information above is free, to recieve a STOP THE BLEED® certification, there is a nominal fee to cover materials and manikin wear.  There is no obligation to obtain the certification, but it is encouraged that you to choose the practial training to be confident in your understanding of the material.
