The Heartsaver First Aid / CPR / AED program covers Adult, Child, Infant CPR with AED and Choking with various First Aid topics.

Designed for employee training in workplaces, scouting leaders, coaches, and non-medical home care workers. For employees and/or designated first responders who have a duty to respond to a first aid or cardiac emergency because of job responsibilities or regulatory requirements.

What is covered

  • Adult first aid (first aid basics, medical and injury emergencies)

  • Adult, child and infant CPR

  • Foreign-body airway obstruction

  • Use of an Automated External Defibrillator (AED)
