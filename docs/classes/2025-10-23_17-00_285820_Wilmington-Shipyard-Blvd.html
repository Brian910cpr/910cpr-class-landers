ARC - Advanced Life Support (ALS) - In-Person

Instructor-led ALS training for new or expired providers. This American Red Cross (ARC) Advanced Life Support (ALS) course is designed for healthcare professionals who need comprehensive, hands-on training to manage life-threatening medical emergencies. This fully in-person course follows evidence-based guidelines to enhance your ability to assess, recognize, and provide critical interventions in adult patients.

🚑 Who Should Take This Course?

This course is ideal for:

  • ✅ Emergency responders, paramedics, and EMS personnel

  • ✅ Nurses, physicians, and advanced care providers

  • ✅ ICU, ER, and critical care professionals

  • ✅ Any healthcare provider needing ALS certification

    • 📌 Course Topics Include:

    • Recognition & management of respiratory and cardiac emergencies

    • Advanced airway management techniques

    • ALS response algorithms and systematic patient assessment

    • High-performance CPR and AED integration

    • Critical team communication and emergency interventions

      • 🎓 Course Includes:

      • ✅ Instructor-led classroom training & hands-on skill stations

      • ✅ Scenario-based learning for high-risk patient care

      • ✅ 2-year ARC ALS Provider certification upon successful completion

        • 📢 Certification Requirements:

        Participants must successfully complete:

        • 💡 All required in-class skills testing and evaluations

        • 📝 Final written exam covering ALS guidelines & protocols

          • 📖 Required Textbook:

          The ARC ALS Provider Manual is required for this course. You can choose:

          • 📕 Physical Textbook – Available for purchase at registration

          • 📘 Digital eBook – Immediate access upon registration

            • 🩺 Add a BLS Renewal:

            Need to renew both certifications? Add BLS renewal during registration and complete both certifications in one session.
