BLS
(In-Person Class)

Basic Life Support class taught fully in-person, designed for healthcare providers who require hands-on practice and testing.

BLS
(Online + In-Person Skills)

Blended option where the cognitive portion is completed online and followed by an in-person hands-on skills check session.

BLS & First Aid
(Online + In-person Skills)

Combined CPR, AED, and First Aid online course with follow-up in-person skills assessment. Ideal for workplaces requiring OSHA-approved certifications.

ACLS
(In-Person Class)

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support course taught in-person, ideal for hospital staff and emergency response teams.

ACLS
(Online + In-Person Skills)

ACLS Heartcode course with online theory and in-person skills testing, approved for both initial and renewal certifications.

PALS
(In-Person Class)

Pediatric Advanced Life Support course focused on child and infant emergencies, delivered fully in-person.

PALS
(Online + In-Person Skills)

Online PALS content followed by mandatory in-person skills session. Great for providers needing schedule flexibility.

CPR/AED
(In-person)

Classroom-based CPR and AED certification appropriate for non-medical professionals and workplace compliance.

CPR/AED/First Aid
(In-Person)

Teaches lifesaving CPR and AED skills along with first aid for injury and illness. Ideal for schools, fitness centers, and businesses.

CPR/AED/First Aid
(Online + In-Person Skills)

Complete online course paired with a short in-person practical. OSHA-acceptable and convenient for busy professionals.

Family & Friends CPR
(In-Person)

Non-certification course for parents, grandparents, and community members who want to learn basic CPR skills.
