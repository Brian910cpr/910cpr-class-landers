Policies & Course Terms

Last updated: 2025-09-18 • Questions? Email info@910cpr.com or call (910) 395-5193.
These policies apply to classes offered by 910CPR at our Wilmington and Burgaw locations, as well as onsite classes for organizations.

Rescheduling

You may reschedule up to 24 hours before your class start time at no charge. If you are running late on the day of class, call (910) 395-5193; we’ll do our best to help.

Refunds

Refunds are available up to 48 hours before class. Within 48 hours, we offer a one-time reschedule to a future date. Missed classes without notice are not refundable.

Attendance & ID

Please arrive 10 minutes early and bring a government-issued photo ID. For blended courses, bring your valid online completion certificate (HeartCode/Red Cross/HSI, as applicable).

Certification Delivery

eCards are typically issued the same day after successful completion. Official processing may take up to 1–2 business days depending on the certifying body.

Prerequisites

Some renewals require a current certification card or a completed online module prior to skills. Please review your course page for any prerequisites.

Safety & Conduct

We maintain a safe, respectful learning environment. If you require accommodations, contact us before your class so we can prepare.

American Heart Association Brand Use

910CPR complies with the AHA Program Administration Manual (PAM). We are not the American Heart Association and do not represent ourselves as the AHA. “AHA,” “BLS,” “ACLS,” and “PALS” are trademarks of the American Heart Association. Courses are delivered only by appropriately credentialed instructors and follow required course lengths and prerequisites.

Accessibility

Our locations are ADA accessible. If you need an accommodation, please contact info@910cpr.com in advance so we can arrange assistance.

Contact

910CPR • (910) 395-5193 • info@910cpr.com

Privacy

We collect only the information necessary to register you for class and issue your certification. We do not sell personal information. For privacy questions, email info@910cpr.com.
