910CPR Class Policies

Effective Date: 6/11/2025

Terms & Conditions of Enrollment

  • Eligibility: Choose the correct class for your job or program (AHA, ARC, HSI). Ask us if unsure.
  • Payment: Due at registration.
  • Manuals: You must have the required course manual before, during, and after class.
  • Online/Blended: Bring your completion certificate to the skills session.
  • Completion: Attend all sections, pass any tests, and demonstrate skills competency.
  • Physical ability: Be prepared to kneel and perform compressions on the floor; request accommodations in advance.
  • Cards: Issued by AHA/ARC/HSI; typically emailed within 1–5 business days (allow up to 20).
  • Conduct: We may remove disruptive/unsafe participants without refund.
  • Jurisdiction: North Carolina (New Hanover County).

Refund & Rescheduling Policy

  • Refunds: Cancel ≥72 hours before class for a full refund. No refunds for no-shows/late arrivals or missing required docs.
  • Reschedule: One free reschedule with ≥24 hours' notice; use within 30 days.
  • Provider changes: If we cancel (weather, low enrollment, emergency), choose a full refund or transfer.
  • Online codes: Non-refundable once issued.
  • Groups/Onsite: May have additional terms.

Privacy Policy

  • We collect: Name, email, phone, class details, preferences, payment confirmations, and blended-course certificates.
  • Use: Register you, issue eCards (AHA/ARC/HSI), confirmations, reminders, support.
  • Protect: We don’t sell your data. Shared only as needed to issue certification or by law.
  • Retention: Records kept ≥3 years or as required.
  • Your rights: Request a copy/correction at info@910CPR.com.
  • Cookies: Basic analytics only.
