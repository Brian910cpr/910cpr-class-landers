AHA HeartCode ACLS – Online Course + Skills Session

The American Heart Association HeartCode ACLS course provides a flexible blended learning option for healthcare professionals who need Advanced Cardiac Life Support certification.

Students complete the interactive online HeartCode ACLS course at their own pace and then attend a short in-person skills session with an AHA instructor to demonstrate hands-on resuscitation skills.

How the HeartCode ACLS Course Works

  • Complete the HeartCode ACLS online course
  • Schedule and attend a short in-person skills session
  • Receive your American Heart Association ACLS Provider eCard

Course Format

This blended course combines online learning with an instructor-led skills evaluation. The in-person session typically lasts between 45 and 90 minutes.

Certification

Upon successful completion of the skills session, students receive the American Heart Association ACLS Provider eCard, valid for 2 years.
