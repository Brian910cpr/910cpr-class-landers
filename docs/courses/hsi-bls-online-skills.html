HSI BLS Certification – Online Course with In-Person Skills Session

The HSI Basic Life Support (BLS) course prepares participants to recognize life-threatening emergencies and provide high-quality CPR using current resuscitation science and international treatment recommendations.

This blended learning format allows students to complete the knowledge portion online followed by an in-person skills session with an instructor.

Who This Course Is For

  • Healthcare workers whose employer accepts HSI BLS certification
  • Dental offices and outpatient clinics
  • Emergency response teams
  • Professional rescuers
  • Anyone needing professional-level CPR training

What You Will Learn

  • High-quality CPR for adults, children, and infants
  • Use of an Automated External Defibrillator (AED)
  • Team-based resuscitation principles
  • Relief of choking
  • Scene safety and patient assessment

Course Format

This course uses a blended learning format:

  • Part 1: Online self-paced training modules
  • Part 2: In-person hands-on skills evaluation with an instructor

Certification

Students who successfully complete both parts receive an HSI Basic Life Support certification card valid for two years.

Choosing the Right BLS Course

Many hospitals and healthcare employers require the American Heart Association (AHA) BLS certification. Students should confirm employer requirements before enrolling in an HSI BLS course.
