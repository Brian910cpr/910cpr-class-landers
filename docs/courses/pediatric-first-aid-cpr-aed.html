Childcare CPR & First Aid Certification (AHA Pediatric CPR AED)

The American Heart Association Heartsaver® Pediatric First Aid CPR AED course is designed for people responsible for the safety of infants and children. This instructor-led class teaches participants how to recognize and respond to pediatric medical emergencies, injuries, and cardiac arrest.

This course is commonly required for childcare workers, daycare staff, teachers, coaches, camp counselors, and foster parents who must maintain CPR and First Aid certification for employment or regulatory compliance.

Who This Course Is For

  • Daycare and preschool staff
  • Childcare providers and babysitters
  • Teachers and school staff
  • Camp counselors and youth program leaders
  • Foster parents and guardians
  • Coaches and youth sports staff

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive a 2-year American Heart Association Heartsaver Pediatric First Aid CPR AED eCard.
