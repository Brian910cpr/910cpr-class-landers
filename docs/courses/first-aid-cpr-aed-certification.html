First Aid CPR AED Certification – AHA Heartsaver

The American Heart Association Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED course teaches individuals how to recognize and respond to common medical emergencies, injuries, and life-threatening situations.

This blended learning course combines self-paced online learning with a short in-person skills session led by an AHA instructor.

Blended Learning Format

Step 1 – Online Coursework

Students complete the self-paced Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED online course before attending the skills session. Estimated time: approximately 3 hours.

Step 2 – In-Person Skills Session

Students attend a hands-on session with an instructor to practice and demonstrate CPR, AED use, and first aid skills. Estimated time: approximately 2 hours.

Certification

After completing both parts, students receive the American Heart Association Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED eCard valid for 2 years.
