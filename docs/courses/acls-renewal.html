AHA ACLS Renewal – In-Person

The American Heart Association ACLS Renewal course is designed for experienced healthcare professionals who already hold ACLS certification and need to renew before expiration.

This instructor-led course reinforces advanced resuscitation skills using current AHA guidelines. Students review critical ACLS algorithms, airway management techniques, pharmacology, and team-based resuscitation strategies used during cardiopulmonary emergencies.

Who This Course Is For

  • Emergency department nurses
  • ICU and critical care staff
  • Paramedics and advanced EMTs
  • Physicians and physician assistants
  • Respiratory therapists
  • Healthcare providers responsible for advanced cardiac care

Course Length

Approximately 6–8 hours depending on class size and participant experience.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive an American Heart Association ACLS Provider eCard valid for 2 years.
