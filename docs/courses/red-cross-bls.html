Red Cross BLS Certification – In-Person Class

The American Red Cross Basic Life Support (BLS) Provider course is designed for healthcare professionals and first responders who need hands-on BLS training in a classroom setting.

This instructor-led course focuses on high-performance CPR, AED use, airway management, ventilation, and team-based response in adult, child, and infant emergencies.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive a Digital American Red Cross BLS Provider certificate valid for 2 years.
