Become an American Heart Association BLS Instructor

This course prepares qualified candidates to become certified American Heart Association Basic Life Support (BLS) Instructors.

Once certified, instructors can teach AHA BLS Provider courses and Heartsaver CPR and First Aid classes through an authorized training site.

What’s Included

  • AHA BLS Instructor Essentials online course
  • AHA BLS Instructor Manual
  • AHA BLS Provider Manual
  • In-person instructor training session
  • Required instructor monitoring session
  • Optional alignment with our AHA training site
