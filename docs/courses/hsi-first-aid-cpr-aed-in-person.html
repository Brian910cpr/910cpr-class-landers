First Aid CPR AED Certification – HSI In-Person Course

The HSI Adult First Aid CPR AED course provides practical, instructor-led training for individuals who may need to respond to medical emergencies in the workplace, community, or home.

Participants learn how to recognize life-threatening emergencies, provide first aid, perform CPR for adults, children, and infants, and safely use an automated external defibrillator (AED).

Who This Course Is For

  • Workplace safety teams
  • Teachers and school staff
  • Coaches and athletic staff
  • Fitness instructors
  • Residential caregivers
  • Community members who want practical emergency skills

What You Will Learn

  • First aid basics and scene safety
  • Bleeding control and injury response
  • Burns and shock
  • Medical emergencies
  • Adult, child, and infant CPR
  • Relief of choking
  • Use of an Automated External Defibrillator (AED)

Course Format

This is a traditional instructor-led classroom course that includes demonstration, guided practice, and hands-on skills evaluation with a certified instructor.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive an HSI / ASHI CPR AED and Basic First Aid certification card valid for two years.

Workplace Compliance

This training meets OSHA expectations for workplace first aid responders under 29 CFR 1910.151.

Maritime Credential Option

Students pursuing a Merchant Mariner Credential may also receive documentation meeting the U.S. Coast Guard Elementary First Aid and CPR requirement.
