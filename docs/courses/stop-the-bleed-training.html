STOP THE BLEED® Training – Learn to Control Life-Threatening Bleeding

Severe bleeding is the number one preventable cause of death after traumatic injury. The STOP THE BLEED® program teaches ordinary people how to recognize life-threatening bleeding and take immediate action until professional help arrives.

In many emergencies, the person who saves a life is not a doctor or paramedic. It is someone already on scene. Until help arrives, you are the help.

What You Will Learn

  • How to recognize life-threatening bleeding
  • How to apply direct pressure
  • How to pack a wound
  • How to properly apply a tourniquet
  • How to respond safely in traumatic emergencies

Course Options

Participants may complete the STOP THE BLEED® interactive online training for free.

For those who want hands-on experience, a short in-person skills session is available to practice bleeding control techniques using training equipment.

Who Should Take This Training

  • Teachers and school staff
  • Construction and industrial workers
  • Security personnel
  • Church safety teams
  • Coaches and athletic staff
  • Parents and community members
