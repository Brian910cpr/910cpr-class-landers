AHA HeartCode BLS – Online Course + Skills Session

The American Heart Association HeartCode BLS course is a blended learning option for healthcare professionals who need the flexibility of online coursework without skipping the required hands-on skills check.

This format is appropriate for initial certification or renewal when AHA HeartCode BLS is accepted by your employer, school, or licensing body.

How the Course Works

  • Complete the online HeartCode BLS course
  • Attend a short in-person skills session
  • Receive your AHA BLS Provider eCard after successful completion

Who This Course Is For

  • Nurses and nursing students
  • Medical assistants
  • Dental professionals
  • EMTs and paramedics
  • Allied health students
  • Busy healthcare providers who need a flexible certification option

Course Format

This is a blended learning course with a self-paced online module and a short in-person skills session, typically 30–60 minutes.

Certification

Students who successfully complete both parts receive an American Heart Association BLS Provider eCard, valid for 2 years.
