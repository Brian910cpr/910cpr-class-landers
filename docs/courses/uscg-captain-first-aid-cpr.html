USCG Captain License First Aid & CPR Certification

This course provides the training required to meet the U.S. Coast Guard Elementary First Aid and CPR requirement for Merchant Mariner Credential (MMC) applicants and Captain License holders.

The training is based on the American Heart Association Heartsaver® First Aid CPR AED program and includes the documentation required for U.S. Coast Guard compliance.

Certification Issued

  • AHA Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED eCard (valid for 2 years)
  • AMERHA-216 documentation accepted by the U.S. Coast Guard
