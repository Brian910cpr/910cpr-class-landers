AHA BLS Renewal for Healthcare Providers

The American Heart Association BLS Renewal course is designed for healthcare professionals who already hold a current or recently expired BLS Provider certification.

This instructor-led class reviews essential life-saving skills including high-quality CPR, AED use, airway management, and team-based resuscitation.

Who This Course Is For

  • Nurses
  • Physicians
  • EMTs and paramedics
  • Dental professionals
  • Healthcare students

What You Will Learn

  • Adult, child, and infant CPR
  • AED operation
  • Airway management and ventilation
  • Team-based resuscitation
  • Opioid emergency response

Course Format

Instructor-led classroom training with hands-on practice using professional manikins and equipment.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive an American Heart Association BLS Provider eCard, valid for 2 years.

Course Length

Approximately 3 hours.
