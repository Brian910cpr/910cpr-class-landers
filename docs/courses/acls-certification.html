AHA ACLS Provider Certification – In-Person

The American Heart Association Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Provider course prepares healthcare professionals to manage cardiopulmonary emergencies using evidence-based resuscitation protocols and high-performance team dynamics.

This instructor-led classroom course follows the current American Heart Association guidelines and focuses on rapid recognition of life-threatening conditions, effective airway management, pharmacology, and coordinated team-based resuscitation.

Who This Course Is For

  • Physicians
  • Nurses
  • Paramedics and advanced EMTs
  • Respiratory therapists
  • Critical care and emergency department staff
  • Healthcare providers who respond to cardiovascular emergencies

What You Will Learn

  • Recognition and management of cardiac arrest
  • Airway management and ventilation strategies
  • ACLS pharmacology and medication administration
  • Management of acute coronary syndromes and stroke
  • Post-cardiac arrest care
  • High-performance resuscitation team communication

Course Length

Approximately 8 hours depending on class size and experience level.

Certification

Students who successfully complete the course receive an American Heart Association ACLS Provider eCard valid for 2 years.
