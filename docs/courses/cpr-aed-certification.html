CPR AED Certification – AHA Heartsaver CPR AED

The American Heart Association Heartsaver CPR AED course teaches individuals how to respond confidently to cardiac and breathing emergencies. This instructor-led class is designed for non-medical professionals who need CPR certification for work, school, or personal preparedness.

Students learn essential life-saving skills including adult, child, and infant CPR, how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED), and how to respond to choking emergencies.

Who This Course Is For

  • Teachers and school staff
  • Fitness instructors and coaches
  • Childcare providers and babysitters
  • Construction and workplace safety teams
  • Lifeguards and camp counselors
  • Parents and family members
  • Anyone who wants CPR training

Course Length

Approximately 2 hours.

Certification

Upon successful completion, students receive the American Heart Association Heartsaver CPR AED course completion card valid for 2 years.
