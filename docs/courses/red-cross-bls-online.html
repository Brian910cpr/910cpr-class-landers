Red Cross BLS Certification – Online Course with Skills Check

The American Red Cross Basic Life Support (BLS) blended learning course gives students a flexible way to complete BLS certification by combining online coursework with an in-person skills check.

This format is ideal for healthcare professionals, students, and staff who need Red Cross BLS certification but prefer to complete the knowledge portion on their own schedule before attending a hands-on evaluation.

How the Course Works

  • Complete the American Red Cross BLS online course and test
  • Schedule and attend an in-person ARC BLS skills check
  • Present your online completion certificate at the session
  • Receive your Red Cross BLS digital certificate after successful completion
