HSI First Aid/CPR/AED

HSI First Aid/CPR/AED for workplace, school, and professional requirements, including practical recognition of choking, opioid emergencies, stroke, seizures, asthma, heat illness, burns, and common injuries.

First AidFirst AidCPR/AED
  • For OSHA-style workplace first aid, CPR, and AED requirements.
  • Strong fit for business, industrial, school, and staff training needs that benefit from scenario-based response practice.
  • Topics can include naloxone access, CPR with breaths for respiratory arrest, FAST stroke recognition, seizure safety, asthma inhaler assistance with spacer preference, heat illness cooling, burns, eye injuries, ticks, stings, poison ivy/oak, and pulse oximetry limitations.
