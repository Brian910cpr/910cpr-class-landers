Student Help

After Your Class

Certification records are processed after the instructor completes the class record.

  • Most certification cards are delivered electronically after the course record is completed.
  • Check the email address used for registration, including spam or junk folders.
  • The delivery email may come from the certification provider rather than directly from 910CPR.
  • AHA, HSI, and American Red Cross records may use different provider systems. 910CPR can help route the question, but does not publish private roster or student information on this page.

Employer Requirements

Were You Sent by an Employer?

Use the exact certification name from your employer, school, licensing board, email, or form when choosing a class.

  • BLS is not the same as general CPR/AED. Healthcare, dental, and clinical roles often require BLS.
  • ACLS and PALS are advanced certifications and are different from BLS.
  • If the wording is unclear, contact 910CPR before registering so you do not choose the wrong course.
