Little Lifesavers – Infant CPR & Choking for Parents & Grandparents

Learn simple, proven steps to help your baby in an emergency. In one friendly session, you’ll practice infant CPR and choking relief so you can act with confidence when seconds matter.

🍼 Infant CPR basics
  • Recognize when to start CPR

  • Breaths that actually help

  • Back slaps & chest thrusts (infant)
  • When to call 911 and what to say

  • Safe sleep & common home risks
  • When to use your pediatrician vs. EMS
  • How to stay calm and take turns helping
Who it’s for

New Parents, Grandparents, Babysitters & Caregivers

This is a practical, skills-first class designed for families. There is no written test and no certification card—just confidence and muscle memory when it counts.

  • Expecting parents & new parents
  • Grandparents and extended family
  • Babysitters and nanny shares
  • Church nursery & childcare volunteers

If you require certification for this type of work, please consider the AHA Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED course (blended): https://www.hovn.app/910cpr/courses/aha-heartsaver-pediatric-first-aid-cpr-aed-blended/o/cmbspqu900005l7041ug34quy

Common questions

FAQ

  • Is this an official certification? No. This is a non-certification class meant for family readiness. If you need a card for work or school, see our BLS or Heartsaver options.
  • Can we bring our baby? Yes—many parents do. If you’d like a larger group experience, consider hosting an in-home session for your community/group.
  • Do you cover toddler/child CPR? Yes, we can briefly show child adaptations. Ask your instructor and we’ll tailor the session.
  • Do you sell baby-safe first aid kits? We can recommend essentials during class and provide a simple checklist.
